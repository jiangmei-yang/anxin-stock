# Critical Loop 15 Prompt

Review the current product as an AI-startup course judge who is skeptical of
internally generated evidence.

Focus on the external validation funnel:

1. A first-time participant must be able to enter from one URL.
2. The test task must permit keeping, changing, or delaying the original plan.
3. No answer may be preselected or presented as the desired answer.
4. Anonymous task completion, abandonment, comprehension, repeat intent and
   consent must be recorded separately.
5. A stated willingness to view a price must not be counted as paid behavior.
6. The paid-test offer must appear only after feedback and must require a
   separate explicit waitlist action.
7. Do not claim external validation until real target users have submitted it.

Implement the smallest product change that makes this funnel shareable and
auditable. Preserve the no-trading, no-return-promise and privacy boundaries.
