# Critical Loop 21

## Problem observed

The English stock-research shell was ahead of the underlying dossier. Prices and evidence were usable, while Financial health, Quant check, Overview and Open questions still behaved like partially translated placeholders.

## Changes

- Localised the financial-health workflow, including loading and failure states, headline metrics, cross-check formulas, thresholds, reporting-period tables and methodology notes.
- Localised the quantitative verification workflow from question entry through condition confirmation and result interpretation.
- Localised the overview signal board, evidence coverage, thesis state and missing-data states.
- Localised the open-question checklist used by decision review.
- Preserved issuer names, filing titles and retrieved source text in their original language rather than inventing translations.

## Score impact

This closes a major trust gap for English evaluators, but it does not justify a higher overall score by itself. The product remains at **86/100 locally** until the full application, onboarding and evidence pipeline are consistently bilingual and real user evidence exists.
